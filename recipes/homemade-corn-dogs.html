Homemade Deep Fried Fair Corndogs

This recipe is a simple way to bring the fair home! Plus its much easier than you would believe to make your own fair corn dogs at home. This recipe is quick and easy and only takes around 40 mins to complete. You can add whatever you prefer to the batter to also make this recipe your own. This makes a perfect snack for the kids or family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal and flour: This corn dog batter starts with yellow cornmeal and all-purpose flour.
  • Sugar: A pinch of sugar lends a hint of sweetness
  • Baking powder: Baking powder acts as a leavener, which means it helps the batter rise.
  • Salt and pepper: Salt enhances the flavors of the other ingredients.
  • Milk: A cup of milk adds moisture, ensuring a tender coating.
  • Egg: An egg gives the batter enen more moisture and acts as a binder, whch means it helps hold the batter together.
  • Oil: Fry the corn dogs in a neutral oil with a high smoke point, such as vegetable oil.
  • Beef frankfurters: This recipe calls for beef frankfurters, but you can use pork franks, hot dogs, or whatever you prefer.

Steps

  1. Make the batter by combining the dry ingredients, then stirring in the milk and egg.
  2. Skewer the frankfurters and roll in the batter.
  3. Fry the corn dogs in hot oil until lightly browned.
